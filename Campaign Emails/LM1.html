Thank you for your interest in The Genius of One Course. We believe God's answer for this fractured world lies in how we, as Christians, reflect His love to those around us. And it's evident we have work to do.

A recent study performed by Barna uncovered the following staggering statistics regarding U.S. Christians on a few of the topics we cover in The Genius of One Course.

  • 25% of pastors say conflict at church is occasionally or rarely resolved in a healthy way.
  • Only 12% of pastors report their congregations having a great culture of forgiveness.
  • The conflict resolution style most practicing Christians naturally pursue is conflict avoidance.

Over five sessions, join us in studying what Scripture has to say about unity, conflict resolution, boundaries, forgiveness, and spiritual gifts as we take steps together to apply practical tools curated by Christian counselors and pastors to help us live these concepts out.
